🎓 Knife Academy
Lesson 1

Knife Anatomy

Understand the tool in your hand.

  • Tip: Delicate cutting and piercing.
  • Spine: Top thick edge, unsharpened.
  • Edge: The working part of the blade.
  • Heel: Rear part of the edge for chopping hard items.
  • Handle: Where you grip.

🧼 Care & Maintenance

How to Clean

Proper cleaning extends the life of your board.

  • Wash immediately after use with warm soapy water.
  • DO NOT submerge or soak in water.
  • DO NOT place in the dishwasher.
  • Dry completely with a towel standing upright.

Deep Clean & Deodorize

  • Sprinkle coarse salt over the board.
  • Use half a lemon to scrub the salt across the surface.
  • Let sit for 5 minutes, scrape off, rinse, and dry.

Board Conditioning

For wooden boards, oiling is essential every 3-4 weeks.

  • Use food-grade mineral oil.
  • Apply a generous coat and let it soak overnight.
  • Wipe off excess in the morning.
